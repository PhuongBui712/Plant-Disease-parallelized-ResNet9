This dataset is downloaded from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vipoooool/new-plant-diseases-dataset.

This dataset is organized into three folders: train, valid, and test. Each folder contains images of different plant diseases. The dataset organization is represented like following:

data/
└── New-Plant-Diseases-Dataset/
    ├── test/
    ├── train/
    └── valid/
